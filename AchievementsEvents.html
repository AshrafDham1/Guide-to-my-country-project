UAE's Major achivement

UAE's Major achivement

Embark on a journey through the remarkable achievements that have defined the United Arab Emirates as a beacon of innovation, resilience, and progress. From pioneering advancements in technology and infrastructure to groundbreaking initiatives in sustainability and diplomacy, the UAE's unwavering commitment to excellence has propelled it onto the global stage. Join us as we delve into the stories behind the nation's major achievements, each a testament to the visionary leadership and indomitable spirit that continue to shape the UAE's inspiring narrative of success.


Dubai Wins the Bid To Host Expo 2020

At the 154th Bureau International des Expositions in 2013, Dubai was announced the winner to host Expo 2020. Defeating rival bids from Turkey's Izmir, Brazil's Sao Paolo and Russia's Yekaterinburg following three rounds of closed voting, the Emirate become the first Arab and Middle Eastern city to host the Expo in 2020. The global event is a 100-year-old platform where many of the world’s most important inventions were unveiled and launched.

Launching an Artificial Intelligence Strategy

Dubai’s ruler, Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid, announced the launch of the UAE’s first ever Artificial Intelligence Strategy in 2017 with the notion to be the first of it’s kind in the world to make the UAE the first in the field of AI investments in various sectors including transport, health, space, renewable energy, water, technology, education, environment, and traffic. This particular strategy is set to be established under the UAE’s Centennial 2071 plan. Following the launch of this plan, 27-year-old Sultan Al Olama was appointed the world’s first Minister of Artificial Intelligence.

UAE is Proclaimed as the Most Generous Country in the World

Having been named the most generous country in the world for 2 years running, the UAE contributed 1.26% of its gross national income to development aid, according to data from the Development Assistance Committee of the Organization for Economic Co-operation and Development. The Arab country was closely followed by Sweden at 1.09 percent and Luxembourg at 1.06 percent

Appointing a (Female) Minister Of Happiness

UAE is all about equality and it does not matter your race or gender and so Ohood Bint Khalfan Al Roumi was appointed the UAE’s first ever Minister of Happiness in 2016, and has the responsibility to use her powerful position to promote and sustain happiness and positivity throughout the Arab state.

The Launch of the UAE Space Agency

The UAE established its first Space Agency in 2014, with an objective to launch a probe to Mars. Announcing the exciting project, Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid, the Prime Minister of the UAE and Ruler of Dubai tweeted that the UAE hopes to send the first Arab spaceship to Mars by 2021, signing a deal with NASA in 2016, the country will work together with them to achieve their goals to reach Mars.

The UAE reaches Mars for the first time with its Hope probe

A probe from the United Arab Emirates successfully reached Mars earning the country a spot in a small group of spacefaring powers that have sent spacecraft to study the fourth planet from the Sun. The UAE hopes the mission will yield key discoveries on Martian weather patterns and catalyze a new science and technology sector as it looks to wean its economy from oil dependence

UAE's Annual Events

UAE's Annual Events

The UAE's eventful calendar attracts visitors year-round, with the Global Village serving as a major draw. Additionally, the country hosts several permanent exhibition spaces, including the Abu Dhabi National Exhibition Centre, Dubai World Trade Centre, and Expo Centre Sharjah. These venues welcome thousands of visitors annually, facilitating cultural exchange and economic activity. With its diverse offerings, from business conferences to cultural festivals, the UAE continues to be a top destination for travelers seeking unique experiences.
